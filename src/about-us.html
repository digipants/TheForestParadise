the forest paradise resorts

Immerse Yourself in the Untouched Beauty of Nature at Forest Paradise Resort

Welcome to Forest Paradise Resort, a hidden gem nestled in the lush greenery of Ramnagar, at the doorstep of the iconic Jim Corbett National Park. Here, we offer more than just a place to stay — we provide a sanctuary where nature’s beauty and modern luxury coexist in perfect harmony. Whether you're seeking adventure in the wild or a peaceful retreat, our resort is designed to deliver an unforgettable experience.

At Forest Paradise Resort, we offer luxurious accommodations, an inviting swimming pool, a multi-cuisine restaurant, and a rejuvenating spa. Guests can enjoy exciting activities like jungle safaris, nature walks, and bonfires. Our conference and banquet facilities are perfect for events, blending comfort, adventure, and relaxation amidst nature's beauty.

Your Gateway to Tranquility, Family Fun, and Adventure

01

Experience the tranquility of nature at Forest Paradise Resort, nestled near Jim Corbett National Park. Our serene environment invites relaxation, allowing guests to unwind amidst breathtaking landscapes, soothing forest sounds, and fresh air, making it an ideal retreat for nature lovers seeking peace and rejuvenation.

02

Enjoy unparalleled comfort at Forest Paradise Resort with luxurious accommodations designed for relaxation. Our rooms feature modern amenities and stunning views, ensuring a restful stay. Indulge in exquisite dining options, a refreshing swimming pool, and rejuvenating spa treatments, blending comfort seamlessly with the beauty of nature.

03

Embark on thrilling adventures at Forest Paradise Resort! Explore Jim Corbett National Park through exciting wildlife safaris, guided nature walks, and bird-watching tours. With various activities available, our dedicated staff curates personalized experiences that ensure unforgettable memories, all set against the stunning backdrop of nature's beauty.
